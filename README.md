## DiscordThemes

If you do not have `BetterDiscord` installed click [here](https://rauenzi.github.io/BetterDiscordApp/) and follow directions. 
<br>
1. After you have installed BetterDiscord, download any of the themes you like and place them in the `themes` folder under the BetterDiscord directory. 
1. When you open up Discord again. Go into `User Settings` and click on `Themes` on the sidebar menu. 
1. A list of themes will show up and you can turn on whichever one you like to load up the theme.
